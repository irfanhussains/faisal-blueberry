BLUEBERRY ALMOND DREAM

A smoothie that tastes like a dream. Add G ESSNTL powder
to get a complete protien with essntial amino acids.

Ingredients

1 scoop G ESSNTL Whey isolate Protien Powder - Vanilla

1 Cup Blueberries

1/4 Cup Quick Oats (dry)

1 Tbsp Almond Butter

1/2 Cup Ice

1/2 Cup Water

Instructions

Add all ingredients to blender and blend to desired consistency.
